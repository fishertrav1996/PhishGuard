Frequently Asked Questions

What is PhishGuard?

PhishGuard is a cybersecurity training and testing platform focused on helping healthcare organizations defend against phishing, ransomware, and other email-based attack vectors.

How does PhishGuard help prevent ransomware?

Most ransomware attacks begin with a successful phishing email. PhishGuard helps reduce this risk by training users, sending simulations, and providing analytics on your organization's susceptibility to email threats.

Is PhishGuard built specifically for healthcare?

Yes. While any organization can benefit, PhishGuard is designed around HIPAA-regulated environments and the threat patterns most common in hospitals, clinics, and healthcare systems.

Do I need technical experience to use PhishGuard?

No — PhishGuard is built to be user-friendly. Administrators can manage campaigns, view reports, and deploy content without needing advanced technical skills.

Does PhishGuard store patient data or PHI?

No. PhishGuard never stores or processes PHI or sensitive patient information. The platform focuses solely on employee awareness and email defenses.

Can PhishGuard integrate with our existing email system?

Yes. PhishGuard works with common email platforms such as Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace. Integration is lightweight and does not require major system changes.

Is support available?

Absolutely — PhishGuard offers ongoing support, onboarding assistance, and help with training campaigns if needed.
